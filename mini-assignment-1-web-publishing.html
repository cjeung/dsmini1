FOOD

This is from Sushi Enya, a sushi spot local to Sawtelle that I recently visited to celebrate one of my friends' birthday! This was their Chef #1 special roll. It was super yummy and very visually appealing, but it was a bit expensive. Not for the everyday college student budget but definitely for a nicer occasion when you're willing to splurge a little!

This is from Yup Dduk, a popular spicy rice cake spot in K-Town! This spot is really well known for their incredibly spicy tteokbokki, which are a few servings each. I went with a group of six friends and we got a two bowls to share; one challenge level spicy and one mild bowl. The mild was already pretty spicy and there was even a warning on the menu for it! Overall, a very tasty and customizable experience since you get to choose what you add to your bowls.
